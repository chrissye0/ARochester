Happy Earth Tea

ABOUT

Happy Earth Tea was established in 2011 by Niraj Lama and his wife Mary Boland. Niraj hails from Darjeeling, India – a region that produces some of world’s finest black teas. Before Happy Earth Tea, the family owned and operated Darjeeling Tea Exclusive based in Darjeeling. After the family resettled in the US, Darjeeling Tea Exclusive gave way to Happy Earth Tea. Happy Earth Tea is committed to curating some of the best organic teas found in the world.
