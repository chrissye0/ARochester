ARochester

About

ARochester is an augmented reality project that brings Rochester’s culture and history to life. By using your phone or tablet, you can explore iconic sites around the city in a completely new way—through immersive, interactive AR experiences. Visit different locations around the city to find new posters and learn what makes it special to others in the community through anecdotal tales.

Project Description

This site was created for IGME 480 to highlight the intersection of technology and storytelling. Our team selected notable locations throughout Rochester and enhanced them with augmented reality content that’s accessible directly in a browser—no app download required.
